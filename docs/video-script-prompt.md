# Koda Demo Video — AI Generation Prompt

Paste the prompt below into HeyGen, Synthesia, or any AI video tool.
If recording manually with Loom, use it as your scene-by-scene guide.

Target length: 90 seconds.
Tone: casual, confident, like showing a coworker a cool tool.

---

## Prompt to paste into your AI video tool

Create a 90-second product demo video for a Windows app called Koda.
Tone: friendly and practical — like a colleague showing you something useful,
not a corporate sales pitch. No hype, just show what it does.

Use a screen recording style with a voiceover. Show a Windows 11 desktop
throughout. The narrator speaks in a calm, clear voice.

---

### Scene 1 — Hook (0:00–0:10)
Screen: Someone typing slowly in Gmail, one finger at a time.
Narrator: "What if you could just... talk instead of type?"

---

### Scene 2 — The hotkey moment (0:10–0:25)
Screen: Same Gmail window. A small recording indicator appears.
The user holds Ctrl+Space, speaks, releases. Text appears instantly in the email.
Narrator: "Koda is a push-to-talk transcription tool for Windows. Hold
Ctrl+Space, say what you want to write, let go — and it types it for you.
In any app. No clicking, no switching tabs."

---

### Scene 3 — Show it works everywhere (0:25–0:45)
Screen: Quick cuts — Slack message being dictated, then a ChatGPT prompt,
then an Excel cell. Each time: hold Ctrl+Space, speak, text appears.
Narrator: "Slack. ChatGPT. Emails. Excel. Wherever your cursor is,
Koda types there. It's always running quietly in your system tray,
ready when you need it."

---

### Scene 4 — Accuracy and speed (0:45–1:00)
Screen: User dictates a full paragraph at natural speaking speed.
It pastes cleanly, punctuation and all.
Narrator: "It uses Whisper — the same AI that powers most transcription
tools today. Fast, accurate, runs completely on your computer.
Nothing leaves your machine."

---

### Scene 5 — Installation is easy (1:00–1:15)
Screen: Double-click KodaSetup-4.2.0.exe, wizard opens, a few clicks,
Koda appears in the tray.
Narrator: "Setup takes about two minutes. Download, install, done.
No account needed, no subscription, no cloud."

---

### Scene 6 — Close (1:15–1:30)
Screen: Koda tray icon, then back to the Gmail scene from the start —
but this time the email is written in seconds by voice.
Narrator: "If you type a lot — emails, messages, documents — Koda
saves you hours every week. Give it a try."
Text on screen: "Koda — talk, don't type"
Text on screen: "kodaspeak.com"

---

## Notes for the video creator
- Keep the screen demos realistic — normal Windows desktop, nothing staged
- The recording indicator when Koda is listening is a small overlay dot
  (green = recording, disappears on release)
- Music: light background, low volume, nothing distracting
- No need to show Settings or advanced features — keep it simple
- If using an AI avatar, position them in corner overlay, small
